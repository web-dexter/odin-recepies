Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with spices, rice, and meat, or eggs or vegetables such as potatoes in certain regional varieties.

Ingredients

  • Dunar rice - 1 kg
  • Chicken - 1 kg fresh and tender best if possible small chicken.( Under 1.7kg, tender one)
  • Yogurt - 2 cup( 20 rs each)
  • Onions - 250 gm
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 3 tbsp
  • Green chillies - 100 gm
  • Shahi Jeera (Cumin) - 2 tsp
  • Bay leaves - 2
  • Green cardamom - 1
  • Cloves - 8
  • Cinnamon stick - 2
  • Garam masala - no
  • Chopped Fresh green coriander - 2 tbsp
  • Chopped Mint leaves - 3 tbsp
  • Lemon juice - 4 tbsp
  • Ghee - 3 tbsp
  • Milk - no
  • Ezzy chicken tikka masala : 1

Steps to create a delicious Chicken Biryani

  1. Marinate the chicken (at least 2â€“3 hours) in yogurt, biryani chicken tikka masala, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red colour( pinch off).
  2. Soak the Rice in hot water with 2 Bay leaves, 2 Cinnamom sticks, 2 roughly crushed green cardamom, 4 cloves and salt, for about 30 minutes.
  3. Soak the Rice in hot water with 2 Bay leaves, 2 Cinnamom sticks, 2 roughly crushed green cardamom, 4 cloves and salt, for about 30 minutes.
  4. Deep fry the diced onions in refined oil (I use saffola active) till they are crispy and light brown.
  5. Remove the rice after half an hour and keep in such container In a way water gets removed( container or bowl with holes)
  6. Now fry chicken in pan until it gets light roasted just fry pieces( keep marinted gravy for further use).
  7. Now take 100 gm oil in pan , add 1 tbsp zeera, 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste, 1 tbsp dhanya powder, 1tbsp laal mirch powder, 1/2 tbsp turmeric, salt as per taste, 1 grind tomatoe, 100 gm grind green chilly, 2 tbsp mint, chicken tikka marinted gravy ( not pieces only remaining gravy) and roast it for 10 minutes.
  8. Take a big, deep vessel form 1st thin layer of our rice, spread gravy we formed in point 6 along with Chicken pieces evenly, form thick layer of rice on that
  9. Sprinkle a little of the fried onions (about 2 tbsp), yellow rice, corriender, mint and 2 tbsp butter on it.
  10. Sprinkle 2 tbsp coriander leaves and mint leaves, and about 1/2 of the remaining fried onions. Add rice on the top and spread it evenly.
  11. Seal the vessel tightly with Alu Foil and then the lid.
  12. Switch on the gas to the highest flame, and keep the vessel over it. After about 5 minutes, lower the flame to the lowest setting. Let the biryani cook for about 30 min.
  13. Serve hot with coke and salad.
